A timeline of Dr. Stella's life

  • 1956 - Born on October 27 1956 in Lagos state, Nigeria, four years before Nigeria's Independence.
  • 1964-1968 - Attended primary school at Corona School, Yaba, Lagos state.
  • 1969-1974 - Attended Secondary school in Queen's school, Ibadan.
  • 1975-1980 - Graduated from the University of Lagos College of Medicine with a Bachelor of Medicine/Bachelor of Surgery.
  • 1981 - Served her one-year mandatory housemanship at Lagos University Teaching Hospital.
  • 1983 - Spent her residency at Lagos University Teaching Hospital and obtained her West African College of Physicians and Surgeons credential
  • 1984 - Went to London to complete her fellowship in endocrinology at Hammersmith Hospital.
  • 1986 - Ameyo Adadevoh married Afolabi Emmanuel Cardoso on April 26, 1986. The couple had one son, Bankole Cardoso
  • 2012 - Adadevoh was the first to alert the Nigerian Ministry of Health when H1N1 spread to Nigeria.
  • 2014 - Adadevoh correctly diagnosed Liberian Patrick Sawyer as Nigeria's first case of Ebola at First Consultant Hospital in Lagos, Nigeria.
  • 2014 - Adadevoh died from the Ebola virus in quarantine on 19 August 2014 in Lagos, Nigeria.
